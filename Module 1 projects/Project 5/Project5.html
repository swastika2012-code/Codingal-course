Nutritious food

What is Nutritious food? What does Nutritious food includes?

Those foods which provides the essential vitamins, minerals, proteins, fats, and carbohydrates your body needs to function, grow, and heal is known as nutritious food.

Nutritious foods includes:

  • Fruits
    • (Apples, Bananas, Oranges etc.)
  • Vegetables
    • (Spinach, Broccoli, Ladyfinger etc.)
  • Proteins
    • (Mlik, Egg, Meat etc.)

Learn more about healthy and nutritious eating food here: MyPlate Nutrition Guide

Benefits of nutrients food

Eating a nutritious, balanced diet fuels your body and brain, helping you achieve stable energy levels. It is the most effective way to prevent chronic diseases, maintain a healthy weight, and significantly improve your overall physical and mental well-being

Stronger Immunity

Provides the essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants your body needs to fight off infections and heal more quickly if you do get sick.

Enhanced Mental Health & Brain Function

Supports cognitive abilities, focus, and memory while potentially easing the symptoms of depression and anxiety.

Quick Nutrition Note

Try this simple rule: fill half your plate with fruits and vegetables, one quarter with grains, and one quarter with protein.
